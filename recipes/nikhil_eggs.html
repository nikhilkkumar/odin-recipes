Nikhil's Breakfast Eggs

Description

Eggs like you've never eaten before, well maybe. I make my eggs runny and spicy to keep th nutrients there, texture magnificent, and flavor popping.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Calabrian Pepper
  • Bell Peppers
  • Onions
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Bread
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Heat pan to medium and throw olive oil on pan
  2. Chop Onions and Bell Peppers into fine pieces
  3. Turn heat high and throw in minced peppers and onions
  4. Once they are ever so browning, turn heat to low and crack 4 eggs into pan
  5. Break yolks and swirl till color is even across pan
  6. Throw in a spoonful of Calabrian Peppers, a pinch os salt, and dash of pepper
  7. Toast bread and put spread of your choice
  8. Keep on low to medium heat, while swirling and evenly cooking eggs
  9. Once eggs have taken some form, scramble for 2 minutes then turn off pan, keep scrambling for 1 minute after
  10. Empty eggs onto plate and place bread, voila!
