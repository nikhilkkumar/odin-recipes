Curry Chicken Fry

Description

A spicy chicken dish made with classic indian spices and onions. This chicken can be eaten as is, or with my chapati which is my personal favorite pairing.

Ingredients

  • Whole Chicken
  • Onion
  • Chili Powder
  • Cumin
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garam Masala
  • Chilli Pepper
  • Coconut Oil

Ingredients

  1. Chop Onion length wise
  2. Chop Garlic into fine pieces
  3. Set heat to high on pot
  4. Throw coconut oil in pot
  5. Throw in onion and garlic, let simmer for 5 minutes
  6. Cut chicken into parts, skim fat, throw in pot
  7. Throw in all spices!
  8. Cover and let cook -> 15 minutes
  9. Eat!
