Penne Vodka

Description

The classic Penne Vodka pasta that we all know and love but a little more spicy...

Ingredients

  • Penne Pasta
  • Vodka Sauce
  • Plant-based Meatballs
  • Asparagus
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Adobo
  • Red Crushed Pepper
  • Basil
  • Thyme
  • Oregano
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Boil Water, and set 2 pans for medium heat
  2. Coat pans in olive oil
  3. Throw meatballs in one pan, asparagus in the other
  4. Once water is boiling add in Penna pasta
  5. Once meatballs are browning, throw them in the air fryer for 15 minutes
  6. Pour Vodka sauce in empty pan
  7. Season asparagus with Adobo, salt and pepper
  8. Once sauce is simmering, season with all spices
  9. Place meatballs in sauce and lower to heat to medium to let cook
  10. Once penne is Al Dente mix with sauce and meatballs
  11. Serve all together and enjoy!
